===========================================================
               THE ALCHEMIST'S COMPENDIUM
===========================================================

-----------------------------------------------------------
1. HEALTH POTION
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Instantly heals wounds and restores vitality. An essential 
   companion for any long adventure.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron glows gold upon completion.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Mushrooms
   2. Add Golden Carrot
   3. Add Cave Salt
   4. Stir

-----------------------------------------------------------
2. INVISIBILITY POTION
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Grants absolute invisibility for one hour. Ideal for 
   sneaking around unnoticed.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron hisses and the steam completely vanishes.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Moth Wings
   2. Add Spider Eye
   3. Stir
   4. Add Cave Salt

-----------------------------------------------------------
3. STRENGTH POTION
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Doubles the imbiber's physical power for thirty minutes. 
   Handle with extreme caution.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron shakes violently and glows a deep red.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Thick Root
   2. Add Dragon's Breath
   3. Stir
   4. Stir

-----------------------------------------------------------
4. NIGHT VISION POTION
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Allows the drinker to see clearly in pitch-black conditions. 
   Lasts two hours; invaluable for deep cave exploration.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron radiates a pale blue glow.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Mushrooms
   2. Add Cave Salt
   3. Stir
   4. Add Moth Wings

-----------------------------------------------------------
5. POTION OF LUCK
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Tips the scales of fate in your favor for one hour. 
   Highly favored for games of chance, though results may vary.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron pops loudly and the brew turns vibrant green.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Four-Leaf Clover
   2. Stir
   3. Add Golden Carrot
   4. Add Cave Salt

-----------------------------------------------------------
6. POTION OF COURAGE
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Banishes fear and hesitation entirely. Warning: tastes 
   absolutely terrible.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron roars and blazes with a fierce orange flame.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Thick Root
   2. Add Golden Carrot
   3. Stir
   4. Add Newt

-----------------------------------------------------------
7. POTION OF SPEED
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Permits the imbiber to move at twice their regular pace 
   for twenty minutes. Side effects include hiccups and fast talking.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron whistles and begins to spin rapidly.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Newt
   2. Add Spider Eye
   3. Stir
   4. Add Dragon's Breath

-----------------------------------------------------------
8. WATER BREATHING POTION
-----------------------------------------------------------
Description:
   Allows underwater respiration for thirty minutes. Perfect 
   for exploring long-submerged ruins.
Visual Cue:
   The cauldron shifts to a teal hue and bubbles slowly.
Recipe Sequence:
   1. Add Cave Salt
   2. Add Mushrooms
   3. Stir
   4. Add Spider Eye

===========================================================
